# The Technical Skills That Matter When AI Writes the Code
Source: Harvard Business Review, 2025
URL: https://hbr.org/2025/03/the-technical-skills-that-matter-when-ai-writes-the-code

Summary: HBR argues that as AI handles syntax-level coding, engineers must double down on problem framing, client communication, and cross-functional leadership. The article highlights that engineers who can translate ambiguous business problems into clean technical requirements will be the hardest to replace.
