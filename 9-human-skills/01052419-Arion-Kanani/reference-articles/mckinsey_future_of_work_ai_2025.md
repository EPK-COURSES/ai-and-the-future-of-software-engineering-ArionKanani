# McKinsey Global Institute: The Future of Work in the Age of AI
Source: McKinsey & Company, 2025
URL: https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-future-of-work-ai-2025

Summary: McKinsey estimates that while AI will automate up to 30% of current software tasks by 2030, demand for engineers with strong design thinking, ethical reasoning, and stakeholder management will grow by 20%. Human judgment in ambiguous situations remains the key differentiator.
