# ACM: Empirical Limitations of LLMs in Software Architecture
Source: ACM Digital Library, 2025
URL: https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3580000

Summary: This peer-reviewed study tested several leading LLMs against real-world architectural design tasks across legacy enterprise codebases. Results showed AI models failed on 78% of tasks requiring cross-system dependency analysis. Human engineers with systems thinking experience consistently outperformed AI on global optimization problems.
